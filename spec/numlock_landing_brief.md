## What the App Does

NumLock uses Apple's Screen Time API to block apps you choose. Before you can open a blocked app, you solve a set of mental math problems. Get them all right, and your apps unlock for a grace period you choose (15 min, 30 min, 1 hour, or until midnight). When the grace period expires, shields come back automatically.

It's a self-imposed productivity tool for adults — not a parental control app. Think of it as a gym membership for your brain that activates every time you reach for a distraction.

## Target Audience

Adults 25-45 who want to reduce mindless scrolling without feeling punished by aggressive app blockers. People who value brain training, productivity, and self-improvement. Think Duolingo users, not digital detox extremists.
